# Product Context

## Product

Anata Agent is an internal operating system for teams running sales, finance,
fulfillment, and executive work. It is not a marketing site and it is not a
general-purpose analytics dashboard.

## Finance Control

The Finance Control page is a desktop-first operator surface. In one scan, an
operator must be able to answer:

1. How much cash is currently available?
2. What income is confirmed versus merely expected?
3. What must be paid next, and what evidence supports that obligation?
4. What is broken or untrusted in the source data?
5. What single action should happen next?

Finance decisions must remain traceable to Bank CSV, QuickBooks, ClickUp, or a
clearly marked manual exception. The interface must never present a forecast
or an AI recommendation as posted cash.

## Users And Environment

Operations and leadership use the app at a desktop workstation, often during a
short daily money review. They need rapid scanning, defensible numbers, and a
clear path to resolve exceptions without wading through dense reports.

## Voice

Calm, direct, accountable, and specific. State the evidence, the uncertainty,
and the next action. Avoid hype, generic reassurance, or decorative financial
jargon.

## Register

Product UI. Design serves the operational decision, not a visual campaign.
